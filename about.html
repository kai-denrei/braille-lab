About

Braille Lab explores what you can do with the 256 Unicode Braille characters (U+2800 through U+28FF) — animation, expression, data, art.

Credits

DAB (Draw & Animate Braille) is based on the editor by Obaid Nadeem.

Spinners are inspired by the braille spinner demos by esceptico.

Loaders are ported from cli-loaders by agilek, which uses the unicode-animations library. 18 procedural braille spinners re-implemented as vanilla JS.

Emotions is an original module — 20 braille face expressions on a radial picker, built as an experiment in minimalist affect display.

Colophon

Built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JS. No frameworks, no build step, no dependencies. Monospace all the way down.
